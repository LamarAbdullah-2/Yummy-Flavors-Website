Old Fashioned Creamed Goldenrod Eggs

These old fashioned creamed goldenrod eggs are warm and creamy, served over toast, and finished with grated egg yolk, crispy bacon crumbles, and green onions.

4 Servings
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 25 mins
Total: 35 mins
Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 6 large hard boiled eggs
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream, plus more as needed
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce, plus more for serving if desired
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 slices bread, toasted, for serving
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 6 slices bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled

Nutritional Info

Calories: 443

Protein: 21g

Fat: 33g

Carbs: 16g

Cooking Instructions

01

Cut eggs in half and separate yolks from whites. Chop egg whites and set aside. Set yolk halves aside.

02

Melt butter in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Whisk in flour and cook, stirring constantly, 1 minute. Whisk in milk and cream and bring mixture to a boil, stirring constantly. Reduce heat to a simmer. Add hot sauce and salt and pepper to taste. Simmer, stirring often, 3 to 5 minutes. Add chopped egg whites and cook until heated through.

03

Divide creamed eggs over toast. Use a microplane to grate egg yolks evenly over the top or crumble finely with fingers over the top. Top with green onions, crumbled bacon, and more hot sauce.
