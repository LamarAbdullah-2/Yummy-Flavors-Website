Instant Gingerbread Hot Chocolate

A cozy seasonal hot chocolate mix with warm gingerbread spices and a hint of molasses flavor.

20 Servings
Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 5 mins
Total: 10 mins
Drinks

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cocoa
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • pinch cayenne pepper (optional)

Nutritional Info

Calories: 73

Protein: 1g

Fat: 0g

Carbs: 17g

Cooking Instructions

01

Whisk cocoa, white sugar, brown sugar, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, salt, and cayenne together in a bowl.

02

Store the mixture in an airtight container until ready to use.

03

Heat milk until almost simmering and whisk in 2 tablespoons of the mix per cup of milk.

04

Stir until fully dissolved, pour into mugs, and serve hot.
