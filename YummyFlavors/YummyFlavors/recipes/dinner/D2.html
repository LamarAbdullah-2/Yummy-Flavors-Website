Easy Slow Cooker Lasagna Soup

This slow cooker lasagna soup has all the flavors you love from lasagna, thrown together without all the work and cleanup. A comforting and easy meal perfect for busy days.

6 Servings
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 4 hrs 50 mins
Total: 5 hrs
Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 carton beef broth
  • 1 can sliced mushrooms
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 5 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • ricotta cheese (optional)
  • salt and black pepper

Nutritional Info

Calories: 403

Protein: 31g

Fat: 16g

Carbs: 35g

Cooking Instructions

01

Cook ground beef in a skillet over medium heat for 5 minutes, then drain.

02

Place beef in slow cooker and add bell pepper, onion, tomatoes, broth, mushrooms, and seasoning. Cook on low for 4 hours.

03

Add broken lasagna noodles and cook until tender, about 45 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

04

Serve in bowls, top with mozzarella and ricotta cheese.
