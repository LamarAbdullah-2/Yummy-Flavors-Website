Smash Burgers with House Sauce

The famous smash burger with deeply crisped, juicy patties covered with melty cheese, piled on a buttery bun, and topped with a dreamy zip of house sauce.

4 Servings
Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Total: 35 mins
Dinner

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 4 Brioche buns
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • House sauce
  • 1/2 onion, diced or sliced
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato
  • 12 pickle slices

Nutritional Info

Calories: 735

Protein: 28g

Fat: 57g

Carbs: 26.6g

Cooking Instructions

01

Mix melted butter, Worcestershire sauce, fish sauce, and smoked paprika in a bowl. Add to ground beef, mix well, form into 4 balls, and chill for 20 to 30 minutes.

02

Butter the buns and toast them on a skillet or griddle until golden.

03

Prepare the toppings and house sauce, and cut small pieces of parchment paper for smashing the burgers.

04

Heat a cast iron skillet or griddle until very hot. Place burger balls on the surface, sprinkle with salt, and let them sizzle for about 20 seconds.

05

Place parchment paper on top and smash the burgers flat in one swift motion. Cook for a few minutes, then flip and top with cheese. Cook another 1 to 2 minutes until cheese melts.

06

Assemble the burgers with toasted buns, smash burger patties, cheese, lettuce, and house sauce, then serve.
