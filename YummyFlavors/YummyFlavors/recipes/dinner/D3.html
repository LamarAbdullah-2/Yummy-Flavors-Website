Thai Shrimp Curry with Shallot Crispies

This shrimp curry is full of incredible flavor, made with simple ingredients and perfect for a quick and delicious weeknight dinner.

4 Servings
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Total: 25 mins
Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon avocado oil
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • fresh ginger
  • 2 cups vegetables
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 lb shrimp
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • avocado oil (for crispies)
  • shallots
  • panko breadcrumbs
  • cilantro or Thai basil

Nutritional Info

Calories: 488

Protein: 27.7g

Fat: 34.8g

Carbs: 20.5g

Cooking Instructions

01

Heat oil and cook shallots until golden, then add panko and cook until crispy. Set aside with herbs.

02

Wipe the pan and cook oil, curry paste, garlic, ginger, and vegetables for a few minutes.

03

Add coconut milk and simmer until slightly thickened and vegetables are tender.

04

Add shrimp and cook for 2–3 minutes per side until fully cooked.

05

Stir in fish sauce and sugar, adjust seasoning, then serve over rice with shallot crispies.
