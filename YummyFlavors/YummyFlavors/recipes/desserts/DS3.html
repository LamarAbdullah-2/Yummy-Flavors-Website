Two Huge Chocolate Chip Cookies

Just two thick, buttery chocolate chip cookies with crisp edges and a soft, gooey center that melts in your mouth.

2 Servings
Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Total: 15 mins
Desserts

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/3 cup + 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Nutritional Info

Calories: 384

Protein: 3.6g

Fat: 23.5g

Carbs: 42.4g

Cooking Instructions

01

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.

02

Mix butter, brown sugar, white sugar, vanilla, and egg yolk until combined.

03

Stir in flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt, then mix in chocolate chips to form a soft dough.

04

Divide dough into two balls and place on a baking sheet.

05

Bake for 10-12 minutes, then let cookies rest for a few minutes before serving.
