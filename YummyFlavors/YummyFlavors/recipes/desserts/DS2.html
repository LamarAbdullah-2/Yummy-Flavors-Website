No-Bake Lemon Bars

Fluffy, creamy no-bake lemon bars with a buttery shortbread crust and a bright, intense lemon flavor.

16 Servings
Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 0 mins
Total: 4 hrs 15 mins
Desserts

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups shortbread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream

Nutritional Info

Calories: 326

Protein: 4g

Fat: 19g

Carbs: 34g

Cooking Instructions

01

Line an 8x8-inch pan with parchment paper and set aside.

02

Mix shortbread crumbs, melted butter, sugar, and salt, then press firmly into the bottom of the pan.

03

Whisk together condensed milk, lemon zest, lemon juice, and salt until smooth.

04

Beat heavy cream until stiff peaks form, then gently fold into the lemon mixture.

05

Spread filling over crust and refrigerate for at least 4 hours until firm.

06

Lift from pan, cut into bars, garnish with lemon zest, and serve.
