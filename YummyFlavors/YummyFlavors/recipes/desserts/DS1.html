Ooey Gooey Chocolate Pudding Cake

Moist, fudgy yet fluffy chocolate pudding cake with a rich texture and irresistible flavor that will have you wanting seconds.

12 Servings
Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 40 mins
Total: 1 hr 55 mins
Desserts

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 box Devil's food cake mix
  • 1 box chocolate instant pudding mix
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 3 cups confectioners sugar
  • 5 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder

Nutritional Info

Calories: 862

Protein: 6g

Fat: 25g

Carbs: 159g

Cooking Instructions

01

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and grease a Bundt cake pan.

02

Chop the chocolate chips and set aside.

03

Combine cake mix, pudding mix, sour cream, eggs, melted butter, and water. Beat until creamy, then fold in chocolate chips.

04

Pour batter into the pan and spread evenly.

05

Bake for 40–44 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes, then invert and cool completely.

06

Mix butter, sugar, milk, vanilla, and cocoa until smooth and creamy.

07

Frost the cooled cake, slice, and serve.
