Cowboy Butter Snack Mix

This cowboy butter snack mix is a blend of all of your favorite mini crackers, pretzels, corn nuts, and crunch corn snacks slowly baked in smoky cowboy-seasoned butter. It is a flavorful mix for a big party, and has no nuts.

15 to 20 Servings
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Total: 30 mins
Snacks

Ingredients

  • 2 cups bite-size Cheddar cheese crackers
  • 1 cup ranch-flavored Corn Nuts®
  • 2 cups Nacho Bugles®
  • 2 cups Goldfish® crackers
  • 2 cups mini saltine crackers
  • 1 cup mini pretzel twists
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet ranch dressing mix
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoons dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Nutritional Info

Calories: 265

Protein: 4g

Fat: 18g

Carbs: 24g

Cooking Instructions

01

Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).

02

Combine cheese crackers, corn nuts, Nacho Bugles, Goldfish crackers, mini saltines, mini pretzels, ranch dressing mix, melted butter, Worcestershire, red pepper, smoked paprika, dry mustard, garlic powder, and black pepper in a large bowl until evenly coated. Transfer to a large rimmed baking sheet; spread into an even layer.

03

Bake in the preheated oven until lightly toasted and cracker mixture is mostly dry, 20 to 25 minutes, stirring once halfway through.
