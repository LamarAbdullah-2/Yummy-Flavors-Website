No-Bake Energy Bites

These energy bites are a healthy take on traditional no-bake cookies. So good, and good for you! They're a hit with kids and adults and perfect for a quick snack.

24 Servings
Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 0 mins
Total: 1 hr 15 mins
Snacks

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup ground flax seed
  • ½ cup crunchy peanut butter
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Nutritional Info

Calories: 94

Protein: 3g

Fat: 5g

Carbs: 11g

Cooking Instructions

01

Gather all ingredients.

02

Stir oats, chocolate chips, flax seed, peanut butter, honey, and vanilla extract together in a bowl.

03

Roll dough into 24 balls with your hands. Arrange balls on a baking sheet and freeze until set, about 1 hour.

04

Serve and enjoy!
