Mixed Berry Muffins

Not too sweet, fluffy yet dense, and topped with crunchy sugar. These Mixed Berry Muffins are a house favorite!

10 Servings
Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Total: 30 mins
Snacks

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon fine kosher salt
  • 2 cups frozen mixed berries
  • 1/4 cup turbinado sugar
  • 4 tablespoons softened salted butter
  • 1/4 cup honey

Nutritional Info

Calories: 357

Protein: 4.7g

Fat: 17.8g

Carbs: 45g

Cooking Instructions

01

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Whisk the coconut oil, brown sugar, maple syrup, yogurt, eggs, and vanilla together until smooth. Add the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt and stir to form a soft dough.

02

Microwave the frozen berries for one minute. Transfer to a strainer, rinse with warm water, and drain well.

03

Gently fold the berries into the batter with minimal stirring to keep some berries intact.

04

Grease a muffin tin and divide the batter between 9-10 cups. Top with turbinado sugar.

05

Bake for 14-16 minutes until golden, then remove and let cool slightly.

06

Mix butter with honey if desired and serve with warm muffins.
