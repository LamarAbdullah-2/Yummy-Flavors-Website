Pomegranate Fizz

A refreshing mocktail combining tart pomegranate juice, fresh basil, lime, and a bubbly splash of club soda.

1 Serving
Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 0 mins
Total: 5 mins
Drinks

Ingredients

  • 5 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon agave syrup or honey
  • 1 fluid ounce lime juice
  • 3 fluid ounces pomegranate juice
  • 4 fluid ounces club soda
  • ice cubes
  • 1 lime wedge

Nutritional Info

Calories: 133

Protein: 1g

Fat: 1g

Carbs: 35g

Cooking Instructions

01

Mash basil with lime juice and agave in a shaker, then add pomegranate juice and shake well.

02

Fill a glass with ice, strain the mixture, top with club soda, and garnish with a lime wedge.
