Healthy Green Juice

A refreshing detox juice made with kale, cucumber, celery, apples, and ginger—perfect for a healthy boost.

2 Servings
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 0 mins
Total: 10 mins
Drinks

Ingredients

  • 4 stalks celery
  • 2 green apples
  • 1 cucumber
  • 6 leaves kale
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1 piece fresh ginger

Nutritional Info

Calories: 144

Protein: 4g

Fat: 1g

Carbs: 36g

Cooking Instructions

01

Gather all ingredients.

02

Process celery, apples, cucumber, kale, lemon, and ginger through a juicer.

03

Serve immediately or store in a jar in the fridge and shake before drinking.
