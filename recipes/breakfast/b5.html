One Pot Spicy Eggs and Potatoes

One-Pot Spicy Eggs and Potatoes with a homemade tomato simmer sauce, kale, potatoes, eggs, and creamy goat cheese! DEElish!

4 Servings
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 45 mins
Total: 55 mins
Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1/2 white onion, roughly chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 teaspoons ancho chili powder, divided
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 (28 ounce) can tomatoes
  • 5–6 eggs
  • 1 cup diced potatoes
  • 2 cups kale, chopped finely
  • 4 ounces goat cheese

Nutritional Info

Calories: 350

Protein: 17.8g

Fat: 21g

Carbs: 17.4g

Cooking Instructions

01

Heat one tablespoon oil in a deep pot over medium heat. Add onions and saute until very soft. Add garlic, salt, two teaspoons chili powder, broth, and tomatoes. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, then blend until smooth.

02

In the same pot, heat remaining oil over medium heat. Add potatoes and remaining chili powder. Saute until lightly browned. Add sauce back and simmer for 10 minutes.

03

Stir in kale. Make holes and crack eggs into sauce. Cover and cook on low for 10 minutes, then rest 5 minutes. Top with goat cheese and serve.
