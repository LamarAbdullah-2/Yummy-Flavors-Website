Creamy Mushroom Soup

All hail this creamy mushroom soup! Earthy mushrooms, a velvety broth, onions and garlic, with splashes of white wine and cream for a rich and comforting flavor.

4 Servings
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 25 mins
Total: 35 mins
Dinner

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 16 ounces mushrooms
  • 1–2 shallots, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 3 cups broth
  • 1/2–1 cup heavy cream
  • salt and black pepper
  • fresh thyme
  • sourdough bread (for croutons)
  • olive oil (for croutons)
  • garlic (for croutons)

Nutritional Info

Calories: 295

Protein: 8.9g

Fat: 21.8g

Carbs: 14.5g

Cooking Instructions

01

Prepare the croutons and set aside.

02

Heat butter and oil in a pot, then sauté mushrooms and shallots for about 10 minutes. Add garlic and cook briefly.

03

Add flour and stir to coat, then gradually pour in wine and broth, stirring until smooth.

04

Add cream, salt, pepper, and thyme. Let the soup sit over low heat without boiling.

05

Serve warm with croutons on top.
