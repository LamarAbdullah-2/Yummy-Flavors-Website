Million Dollar Mac and Cheese

The recipe is worth a million bucks. This rich and creamy mac and cheese is loaded with multiple cheeses, a smooth sauce, and a crispy buttery topping.

10 Servings
Prep: 30 mins
Cook: 40 mins
Total: 1 hr 15 mins
Dinner

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 ½ cups crushed crackers
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ cups whole milk
  • 1 ½ cups half and half
  • 4 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • vegetable bouillon cube
  • Cheddar cheese
  • smoked Cheddar cheese
  • Colby-Jack cheese
  • cavatappi pasta
  • mozzarella cheese
  • sour cream
  • fresh chives

Nutritional Info

Calories: 594

Protein: 28g

Fat: 40g

Carbs: 32g

Cooking Instructions

01

Preheat oven to 350°F and grease a baking dish.

02

Toast crushed crackers with butter in a skillet, then set aside.

03

Cook flour and butter in a pot until lightly toasted.

04

Add milk and half-and-half, whisking until thickened.

05

Mix in mustard, salt, garlic powder, paprika, and bouillon.

06

Add cheeses gradually and stir until melted and smooth.

07

Stir in cooked pasta and spread half into baking dish.

08

Add cheese and sour cream layers, then remaining pasta.

09

Top with remaining cheese and cracker mixture.

10

Bake for 25–30 minutes until bubbly, let rest, garnish with chives, and serve.
