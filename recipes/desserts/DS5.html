House Favorite Cinnamon Rolls

Soft, pillowy cinnamon rolls made with brioche dough and topped with rich cream cheese frosting in every bite.

8 Servings
Prep: 12 hrs
Cook: 20 mins
Total: 12 hrs 20 mins
Desserts

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup lukewarm water
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 5 tablespoons white sugar
  • 5 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 ounces cream cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/3 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt

Nutritional Info

Calories: 231

Protein: 4g

Fat: 10.7g

Carbs: 30.1g

Cooking Instructions

01

Mix water and yeast in a bowl. Add melted butter, honey, eggs, salt, and flour and mix to form a sticky dough.

02

Cover and let the dough rise at room temperature for 1-2 hours until doubled.

03

Deflate dough gently and refrigerate for at least 3 hours until easier to handle.

04

Roll chilled dough into a rectangle and brush with melted butter.

05

Sprinkle with sugars and cinnamon evenly over the dough.

06

Roll into a log and cut into 8 rolls, then place in a baking dish.

07

Let rolls rise again in a warm oven for 45 minutes, then bake at 350 degrees for 20-22 minutes.

08

Mix cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, vanilla, milk, and salt until smooth.

09

Spread frosting over warm rolls and serve.
