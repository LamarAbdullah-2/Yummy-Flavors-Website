Teriyaki Salmon Bowl

For this teriyaki salmon bowl, salmon is baked for 15 minutes with a 4-ingredient homemade teriyaki sauce using soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger, then served in a bowl with rice, red cabbage, carrots, and radishes.

1 Servings
Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Total: 30 mins
Lunch

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 small clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh ginger
  • 1 (6 ounce) salmon filet
  • 1/4 cup grated carrots
  • 2 radishes, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional)
  • 1 green onion, thinly sliced (optional)

Nutritional Info

Calories: 780

Protein: 50g

Fat: 22g

Carbs: 96g

Cooking Instructions

01

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) and spray a baking dish with cooking spray.

02

Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger in a small bowl. Place the salmon in the prepared baking dish, skin side down, and pour the teriyaki sauce over.

03

Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 12 to 15 minutes.

04

Place rice in a bowl, top with salmon, and spoon over teriyaki sauce. Add cabbage, carrots, and radishes, then garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
