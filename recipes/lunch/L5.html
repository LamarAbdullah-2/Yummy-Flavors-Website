Buffalo Chicken Bowls with Cauliflower Garlic Rice

The ultimate meal prep: Buffalo Chicken Bowls! Spicy buffalo chicken, a fluffy rice and cauliflower rice combo, fresh tomatoes and cucumbers, and a dollop of creamy dill dip.

4 Servings
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Total: 40 mins
Lunch

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast fillets
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 package riced cauliflower
  • 1–2 tablespoons olive oil
  • cucumbers, sliced
  • cherry tomatoes, halved
  • dill dip or dressing

Nutritional Info

Calories: 289

Protein: 30.6g

Fat: 3.8g

Carbs: 34.7g

Cooking Instructions

01

Place chicken, hot sauce, honey, garlic powder, paprika, salt, onion powder, and black pepper in a bowl and marinate for 30 minutes to 2 hours.

02

Cook rice according to package directions.

03

Place cauliflower rice on a sheet pan, drizzle with olive oil, season with salt, and roast at 425°F for 20–30 minutes. Mix with cooked rice.

04

Cook chicken in a hot pan until browned and fully cooked. Let rest for a few minutes.

05

Slice chicken and serve with rice mixture, cucumbers, tomatoes, and a dollop of dressing.
