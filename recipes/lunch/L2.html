Pasta Salad

This pasta salad is nice and colorful, easy to make, and great for summer picnics or parties. The recipe was given to me by a dear friend many years ago, and I've been making it ever since!

6 Servings
Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Total: 30 mins
Lunch

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tri-colored spiral pasta
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 6 tablespoons salad seasoning mix
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • ½ yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped

Nutritional Info

Calories: 400

Protein: 8g

Fat: 25g

Carbs: 39g

Cooking Instructions

01

Gather all ingredients.

02

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 to 12 minutes; rinse under cold water and drain.

03

Whisk Italian dressing and salad seasoning mix together until smooth.

04

Combine pasta, tomatoes, bell peppers, and olives in a salad bowl.

05

Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat.

06

Refrigerate for 8 hours to overnight for best flavor, then serve.
