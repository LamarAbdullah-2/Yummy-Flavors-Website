Baked Potato Soup

This baked potato soup is thick, creamy, and an excellent way to use leftover baked potatoes. It's a family favorite in our house when it's cold and dark outside.

6 Servings
Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 25 mins
Total: 40 mins
Lunch

Ingredients

  • 12 slices bacon
  • ⅔ cup butter
  • ⅔ cup all-purpose flour
  • 7 cups milk
  • 4 large baked potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 ¼ cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Nutritional Info

Calories: 748

Protein: 27g

Fat: 49g

Carbs: 50g

Cooking Instructions

01

Gather all ingredients.

02

Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain on paper towels, crumble, and set aside.

03

Melt butter in a stockpot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Gradually add flour, whisking until well combined. Slowly pour in milk, whisking constantly until smooth and thickened.

04

Add potatoes and onions and bring to a boil, stirring frequently.

05

Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.

06

Stir in bacon, cheese, sour cream, salt, and pepper. Cook until cheese melts, then serve.
